// WHAT IS CHAINIQ

A New Standard for Supply Chain Intelligence

Modern global supply chains manage millions of concurrent shipments across highly complex and inherently volatile transportation networks. Critical transit disruptions — ranging from sudden weather events to hidden operational bottlenecks — are chronically identified only after delivery timelines are already compromised.

ChainIQ fixes this by embedding AI at every layer of the logistics stack. We ingest data from IoT sensors, carrier APIs, weather services, customs databases, and port authorities — processing it in real time to give your team a living, breathing map of your entire supply chain.

// HOW IT WORKS

The ChainIQ Intelligence Pipeline

1. Connect Your Data Sources

Plug in your existing ERP, WMS, carrier portals, and IoT sensor feeds via one-click connectors or REST API. Setup takes under 30 minutes.

2. Real-Time Ingestion & Normalization

Our pipeline processes millions of events per second, normalizing formats from 200+ data sources into a unified schema.

3. AI Anomaly Detection

Transformer-based models continuously monitor your network for patterns that signal upcoming disruptions — weather, congestion, customs backlogs, and more.

4. Predictive Impact Scoring

When an anomaly is detected, our simulation engine forecasts downstream impact across your entire network and scores severity within milliseconds.

5. Optimization & Rerouting

Mixed-integer linear programming solvers compute the optimal alternative route considering cost, time, carrier availability, and risk tolerance.

6. Action & Notification

Your team receives an alert with the recommended action. Accept it in one click or let auto-pilot handle it. All decisions are logged for audit.
